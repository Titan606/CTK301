Veni Vidi Vici
a board game inspired by the games Risk and trivia pursuit. Conquer territories through the use of your worldly funfacts or use your luck in die to steal from your opponents.

Brain Tetris
this is the name my classmate and I decided on a self designed and made controller in a UI/UX class. The concept of this remote is to enable game play with only having to move from neck up with the use of input sensors in front of said player's face. Used with Makey-Makey kit.

Skin mod
Class project done with classmates which involve the use of photoshop and After effects, as well as a third party website to code in the new skins as a mod for a figthing game. Use of Switch console and Super Smash Bros Ultimate.
